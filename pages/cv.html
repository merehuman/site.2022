jessa gillespie is an engineer and creative technologist living in xučyun (Huichin), the unceded territory of the Ohlone people. they hold a masters of science in computer science from depaul university, and they collaborate with artists, educators, and nonprofits on a variety of projects, including game development, virtual reality, and website design and development. more recently, they have been building software tools and games for fun.
